Thanks for using my SpeechToText discord bot :)
Step 1) Run 'get_mics.py'
Step 2) Run VoiceRecognition.py and enter the index of the audio device you
wish to use from 'get_mics.py'
Step 3) Change the token at the top of 'VoiceToTextBot.py' to
that of your bot
Step 4) Run 'VoiceToTextBot.py'
Step 5) Type 'speak' into the channel you want the bot to send your messages
Step 6) Speak into your microphone
